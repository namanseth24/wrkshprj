Weather Bot

Our workshop project, created by Team 12, aims to address two important agricultural needs: pest control and weather monitoring.
The Solar-scarecrow is a innovative solution designed to deter pests from crops by utilizing solar-powered motion sensors and automated scare tactics.
Additionally, our project includes a Weather Station that collects real-time data on temperature, humidity, and rainfall, providing farmers with crucial information for making informed decisions about their crops.
Through this project, we hope to enhance farming practices and contribute to sustainable agriculture.

Through this project, we hope to enhance farming practices and contribute to sustainable agriculture. How it works The Solar-scarecrow Works by detecting the motion of pests within a designated range using solar-powered motion sensors. Upon detecting motion, the scarecrow's automated scare tactics are activated, such as loud noises , effectively deterring the pests from the crops. The Weather Station Collects data on temperature, humidity, and rainfall using sensors, which is then transmitted to a central server for real-time monitoring and analysis by farmers. This help the farmer to maintain their crop according to weather.
